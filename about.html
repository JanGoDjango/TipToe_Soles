About

Tip Toe Soles

We aim to provide comfortable footwear at affordable prices to customers within Australia and around the world. TipToe Soles, is a shoe manufacturing company based out of Texas, USA. The company has been manufacturing and providing the products as a white label to various other brands. The management of the shoe company now wishes to launch its own brand in the market at affordable costs. The management visions the brand to be a to go footwear brand not just in the US but across the world. After US,they are planning to open stores in Australia as well.. The first range of products that will be launched are the comfort or daily wear shoes targeting the 30+ customers.
